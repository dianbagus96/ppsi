About us

Founded in 2018, Temubareng was created for everyone that kept saying:

"I want to event but I don’t know what I must do?"

Fast forward to today and while we have grown significantly (THANK YOU!) our focus hasn’t changed. We now have extensive partnerships across all cities in Indonesia.

We think that event is perfect for creating positive relationships, getting active and having a fun social activity to look forward to each week. We don’t think you should need a whole people of 6 or 7 friends to get joined.

We love creating great event experiences and welcome you to choose an event and join with us.
